⚙️ Firebase Setup Required

Connect Your Database

Enter your Firebase project credentials to get started. These are saved locally on your device.

How to get your config

  1. Go to console.firebase.google.com
  2. Create / open your project → Project Settings
  3. Under Your apps → click </> Web → register app
  4. Copy the values from firebaseConfig into the fields below
  5. Enable Firestore Database (test mode) & Email/Password Auth
